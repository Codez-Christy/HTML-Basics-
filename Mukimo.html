Mukimo Recipe

Ingredients of making mukimo

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Soft Maize
  • 2 hand full of pumpkin leaves
  • spring onions
  • A Spoonful of cooking oil
  • Salt

How to prepare Mukimo

  1. Peel your potatoes
  2. Prepare pumpkin seed by removing stalk andd cutting them to small pieces and blead
  3. Mix the pumpkin leaves , maize and potatoes into a sufuria and add water
  4. place sufuria on the fire to boil and add a pinch of salt
  5. Reduce the heat and mash everything together until bleanded wel the remove from the heat
  6. In a large pot and the spring oil and cook till goldn brown then add the Mashed mixture then mix well
  7. Remove from heat and serve
