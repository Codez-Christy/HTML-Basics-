Ugali recipes


Ingredients and steps of cooking Ugali

Ingredients and tools


  • Maize flour
  • Water
  • Cooking stick
  • Sufuria
  • Source of heat
  • Placing place
  • Cooking oil

Steps of cooking Ugali

  1. Place Sufuria on source of heat.
  2. Add water to the sufuria.
  3. Heat water to boiling point of about 100degrees celcius.
  4. Add cooking oil.
  5. Add atleast 2 cups of maize flour.
  6. Start stirring till a paste is formed.
  7. Continue adding flour till paste hardens.
  8. Start shapping to desired shape.
  9. Leave for approximately 10 minutes before turning.
  10. When ready overturn it onto a plate.
  11. Serve with desired Stew.Enjoy
