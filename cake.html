Cake Recipe

This IS Cake

Ingredients and setup of Cake

Ingredients for cake

  • 3 cups of Flour
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/2 of olive oil
  • 2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla essence
  • 1 1/2cup of sugar
  • 2 bowls
  • A whisk

Steps of preparing Cake

  1. Begin by mixing sugar and olive oil together. Whisk well until light and fluffy with a manual whisker or a fork. Once done, add the beaten eggs and blend well. Beat further so that the mixture turns white and creamy.
  2. Sift together the all-purpose flour and baking soda.Add this to the egg mixture. If required, add a little milk and mix till the batter is fluffy and sofT. Add vanilla essence and blend well.
  3. Now, take a baking tin to cook the cake and grease it well using some olive oil, then sprinkle some all-purpose flour on it
  4. Pour the prepared cake batter into the prepared tin and place it on a stand in a eletric oven
  5. cook at 180 degrees for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Insert a knife or a metal skewer into the cake and if it comes out clean, then the cake is ready
  7. Decorate to you're desire
