Chapati recipe

Ingredients and steps of seting Chapo


this is Chapati

Ingredients and tools


  • 3 cups of flour.
  • 11\2 cups of warm water.
  • 1 tsps of salt.
  • 1 tsp of sugar.
  • Vegetable oil.
  • Rolling Pin.
  • Pan.

Steps on how to prepare chapati.

  1. Add 3 cups of flour in a bowl.
  2. Add salt,sugar,2tsp of oil and 11\2 cups of water in a separate jar stir until the salt and sugar dissolve.
  3. Add the water into the bowl and start mixing.
  4. Knead the dough for 10mins till its non sticky and 2tsps of oil and continue kneading then cover the dough for 40mins.
  5. After 40 mins divide the dough into desired equal part and arrange in aflat surfac dusted with flour.
  6. Dust flour into the flat place and take one balls and start rolling with a rolling pin till the dough is round.
  7. In a hot pan place the rolled dough and fry each side with little oil until golden brown.
  8. Place the cooked chapati in a flat plate and cover with aluminium foil.Repeat this steps for the rest of the dough.
  9. Serve with your Fave stew.
